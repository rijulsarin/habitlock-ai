Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

HabitLock AI is built on a simple principle: your behavioral data is yours. This policy explains exactly what we collect, what we don't, and why.

What data is stored — and where

All data you create in the app — habits, check-ins, miss attributions, obstacle plans, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SQLite). This data is never transmitted to our servers, never sold, and never shared with third parties.

What leaves your device

When you use an AI-powered feature (habit creation, miss attribution, obstacle planning, or the weekly summary), the relevant text from your conversation is sent to our secure server proxy, which forwards it to an AI language model to generate a response. This text is not stored by us after the response is returned. We do not log conversation content.

Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous product analytics (e.g., "a habit was created", "the attribution screen was opened"). These events do not include any personally identifiable information, habit content, or behavioral data. They help us understand which features are working and improve the product.

Crash reporting

We use Sentry to capture crash reports when the app encounters an unexpected error. Crash reports may include device type, OS version, and a stack trace. They do not include habit content or personal data.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app schedules reminders locally on your device using the OS notification system. No notification content is sent to our servers.

No accounts, no registration

The app does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information.

Data deletion

To delete all your data, uninstall the app. Because all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling removes it completely.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date above and note the change in the app's release notes.
